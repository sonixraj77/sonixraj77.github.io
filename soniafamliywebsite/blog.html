Being Blessed with the gift

The gift of a child is the greatest gift of them all

This gift does not come easy and this gift can go bad by making a few mistakes during its maintainance. I dont mean to talk about kids as a comodity however I am just trying to veiw it in an objective manner, just so I can emphasize how important it is to nurture this gift and never treat it bad or even worse abuse it.

The gift of parenthood brings both wonderful things and unimaginably horrible things like learning to go without sleep !!!

These things truly mature us, makes us modest
